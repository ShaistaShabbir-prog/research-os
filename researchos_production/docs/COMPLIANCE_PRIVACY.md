# Compliance and Privacy Notes

ResearchOS handles unpublished academic documents and datasets, so privacy is a product feature.

## Required controls before launch
- Clear user consent for AI processing.
- Option to opt out of model training.
- Data deletion and export.
- EU-region hosting option.
- DPA for labs/universities.
- DPIA template for institutional customers.
- Audit logs for team accounts.
- Separate public/private dataset modes.

## AI transparency
- Clearly label AI-generated feedback.
- Keep human researcher responsible for final submission.
- Never claim guaranteed correctness.
- Show limitations and confidence.

## Risk
If used for formal educational assessment, the product may face higher regulatory scrutiny. Position it as assistive feedback, not automated grading.
