# Business Strategy

## Positioning
ResearchOS is not another AI writing assistant. It is an ethical research workflow system: supervisor feedback, reproducibility checking, dataset documentation, and knowledge graph memory.

## Competitor categories
- Literature tools: Elicit, Consensus, ResearchRabbit, Connected Papers.
- Writing tools: Paperpal, Writefull, Grammarly, SciSpace.
- Repository tools: Zenodo, Figshare, Dataverse.
- Benchmark tools: Kaggle, Papers with Code.

## Differentiation
- Supervisor-style structured critique.
- Results and methodology reviewer.
- Dataset card + reproducibility score.
- Benchmark layer for academic datasets.
- Graph memory connecting papers, datasets, methods, and outcomes.

## Pricing
- Student: €9/month.
- PhD: €19/month.
- Researcher: €39/month.
- Lab: €199/month.
- University: €5k–€50k/year depending on users and storage.

## Moat
The moat is not the LLM. The moat is workflow data, review templates, user trust, institutional integrations, and the research knowledge graph.
