# ResearchOS PRD

## Mission
Build the operating system for modern research: supervisor-grade feedback, dataset reproducibility, benchmark memory, and research knowledge graph.

## Product sequence
1. AI Supervisor: fastest path to users and revenue.
2. Dataset Hub: deeper lab/institution utility.
3. Knowledge Graph: defensible long-term data moat.

## Target users
- MSc students
- PhD students
- Research associates
- Supervisors
- Research groups
- University libraries / research data management offices

## Core promise
ResearchOS does not ghostwrite. It reviews, audits, structures, and helps researchers improve their own work ethically.

## MVP success metrics
- 100 beta users
- 40% weekly active users among thesis users
- 30% of users upload a second document
- 10 paying users within first 60 days
- 3 research groups piloting within 6 months

## Non-goals
- No automatic thesis writing as primary positioning
- No claim of guaranteed acceptance
- No plagiarism bypass
- No high-stakes grading decisions without human oversight
